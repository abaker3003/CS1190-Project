Building a Budget

If you are building a budget for the first time, it is important to know the difference between needs and wants so you can prepare a budget that is customized to you and your net income. You want to evaluate your needs first and your wants seconds.

Needs are the items that you can't live without. These are usually refered to as your four walls:

  1. Mortgage, rent
  2. Groceries
  3. Utilities
  4. Fuel and transportation (car payment, monthly bus pass, monthly uber costs)

Wants are the items that you can live without, but don't want to live without. If your total expenses are greater than your net income, then this is the category you want to look into cutting expenses first.

You may realize you don't need to that gym membership because you don't go to the gym regulary or your apartment complex might have a gym. Maybe you can completely cut out an item, or maybe you find a category that you don't need to give up, an you can just cut back on the monthly cost.

  • Personal care
  • Massages
  • Dining out
  • Luxury items, such as clothes, luxury car payment vs practical car payment
  • Vacations
