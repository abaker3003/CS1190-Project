  • Set realistic goals - Start with a small goal and gradually increase it if you can.

  • Don't wait - The sooner you start saving the more time your money has to grow, through compound interest

  • Get help from a financial advisor - If you are not sure how to get started you can talk with a financial advisor and they can help you create a personalized savings plan.

  • Don't give up - Saving money takes time and discipline, but it's worth it in the long run for working towards your financial goals and getting financial freedom.

  • Don't let inflation fool you - Financial advisors are experts in savings and investing, so it's important to be aware of the effects of inflation.
Compound Interest involves earning interest on your existing interest, leading to accelerated money growth.

If you start investing now through your savings your compound interest can grow significantly, meaning greater returns on your money versus if you were to start later.
