Personal Finance

Why is a budget important?

A budget is important as it can help keep track of your income and expenses, as well as keep track of your financial goals.

Why are financial institutions important?

Financial institutions are important as they provide financial products and services to customers. They offer secure storage for funds, lend money to borrowers and charge interest, and invest reserve funds to generate income.

Why are savings important?

Saving money is important for achieving your financial goals.

Why are credit cards important?

Credit cards are important as Credit allows borrowing and gradual repayment as well as good Credit aids loan approval, lower rates, and better terms.

Why is debt/loans important?

Debt is important because it is money that you borrow from someone else and agree to pay back, usually with interest.Debt can be a good thing if it is used to finance something that will increase your income or assets. Loans are important
